Zombie Survival Resources

We've searched all over the web and put together this collection of our most recommended resources to help you and your buddies make it out of the zombie apocalypse as humans. Preparing for zombies can be a little overwhelming we'd like to help ease the pain. Whether you're thinking of preparing, just began, or have been preparing for a while, these resources will be useful. Here we list our favorite books, movies, websites, and stores.

Zombie Movies!

Yeah I know, movies are fake but there are some great realistic zombie movies that you can learn a thing or two from. Here are our favorites :

1. Zombieland »

This is an awesome zombie movie. Not only does it have good rules and realistic scenarios, it's also a comedy!

2. Dawn of the Dead »

The survivors in Dawn of the Dead barricade themselves in a shopping mall. Sure sounds like an awesome place to call home but we wouldn't recommend it. Watch and learn from their mistakes.

3. 28 Weeks Later »

As the title implies, this story takes place 28 weeks after the last infected human dies. This is a refreshing twist to normal zombie movies. The human race begins repopulation in a government facility but to their surprise they find an infected human still alive. Very realistic plot.

4. Land of the Dead »

The zombies in Land of the Dead are not like the typical slow and dumb zombies, these zombies are Einstein's. They learn from you and communicate with one another. I sure hope real zombies are not like these guys.

5. Shaun of the Dead »

Here is another great comedy. Although it's not meant to be taken too seriously, you can still learn a thing or two about zombie survival.

Zombie Books

People think about zombies so much that they write books about them, some very useful books. Here are our top picks :

1. Zombie Survival Guide »

This book has everything you need to know about zombie survival including how to understand zombie physiology and behavior!

2. 30 Minute Plan »

We included this one because it's a interesting story near post zombie apocalypse when something goes wrong. The best part about this book is that its free on the kindle!

3. World War Z »

This is one of the top rated books. World War Z is a fictional post apocalyptic story of a man who travels the world to document the stories of people who survived the war against the undead.

4. Zompoc »

This is a very detailed manual for surviving the zombie apocalypse. It covers subjects like zombie identification, first-aid, weapons, combat techniques, and lots more.

5. Plan and Prep »

This is not a survival handbook but it provides great survival basics. This book is great for someone just starting off.

Map of the Dead »

This site is soooo cool! It's a google map of your current location but it's not your ordinary google map, this map tells you where to find nearby hospitals, grocery stores, gun stores, military locations, hardware stores, and many more useful locations.

Vegas Zombie Store »

Don't you wish there was a real "physical" zombie apocalypse store you can drive down to and buy all the survival supplies you need? Well there is! The Zombie Apocalypse Store in Las Vegas is open 7 days a week.

Let's Train »

You need more than just weapons to get through the day in a world full of man eating dead people. Find out how to prepare your mind and body, what skills to learn and let's train to become ultimate zombie killing machines.

Weapons 101 »

Choosing a the best zombie weapons can be tricky. Although zombies are "human like" some effective human vs human weapons just won't do the job. Remember, zombies are already dead, half dead at least so you need more than just a little knife to do the trick.
